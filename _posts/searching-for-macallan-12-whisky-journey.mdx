"There's something even better than Balvenie," she said.

That one sentence sparked a fire in my heart. After the sweet, honey-filled revelation of **Balvenie 12**, I couldn't imagine anything being "better" or "smoother." But the name she mentioned was legendary: **Macallan**.

![The Elusive Macallan 12](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQ0sZcFwFUb_WbsIq2Pj1RsGdBfZpOvBEdQjg&s)

## The Elusive Legend: Why Is It So Hard to Find?

I immediately started looking for it. However, I quickly realized why people call it the "Rolls-Royce of Whiskies." Not just because of the price—which was significantly higher than Balvenie—but because of its availability.

In almost every bar we visited, **Macallan 12 was not available by the glass**. They only sold it by the bottle. As a beginner, committing to a whole bottle felt like a massive leap. I had to wait for the right moment.

## A Detour in Bohol, Philippines

That moment seemed to arrive during our vacation to **Bohol, Philippines**. After checking into our resort, we went hunting for a local liquor store.

The selection was limited. Macallan was nowhere to be found. My girlfriend picked out a different 12-year-old single malt, claiming it was also a "good one." At that time, I was so ignorant about whisky brands that I didn't even memorize the name. 

We poured a glass with high hopes, but... **it was a disappointment.** 
Compared to the Balvenie I remembered, the aroma was flat, and the burn was harsher. My soul was still crying out for that missing Macallan experience.

## The Unexpected Invite

We returned to reality after the Bohol trip, the taste of disappointment still lingering. Then, a message arrived from an old friend (Hyung). 

"I'm opening a new bar. We're having a soft opening in a few days. Come over."

Could this be it? Would I finally meet the legend?
