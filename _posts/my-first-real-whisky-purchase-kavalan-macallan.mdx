In the past, I had purchased whisky before, but it was always for business purposes or as gifts for others. I had never spent a single dime buying a bottle strictly for my own consumption. This trip to Taiwan completely changed that.

## Unlocking Kavalan in Taipei

While traveling in Taiwan, I met up with a close friend who lives there. We headed to a dedicated Kavalan tasting bar, where we sampled a wide variety of expressions. Drinking through the lineup, I fell even deeper into the charm of whisky and became completely captivated by the intense profile of Kavalan.

## The TPE Airport Selection

On my way back home, standing in front of the shelves at Taipei Airport (TPE) duty-free, I carefully contemplated my options. After some deliberation, I walked out with two bottles—marking the first time I bought whisky purely for myself.

### 1. Kavalan Triple Sherry
An incredible tropical sherry release that I fell in love with during my stay in Taipei.

![Kavalan Triple Sherry](https://www.kavalanwhisky.com/data/goods/cover/1637303747427196532.png)

### 2. Macallan 12 Year Old Sherry Oak
My long-time all-time favorite sherry expression, which was an absolute must-buy without a second thought.

![Macallan 12 Sherry](https://tony-almanac.pages.dev/images/posts/whisky/macal12color.png)
