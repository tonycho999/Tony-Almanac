Before I truly understood the world of whisky, it was just a status symbol. I had bought famous bottles like Johnnie Walker and Royal Salute in the past, but I didn't actually *know* what I was drinking. It was just "expensive alcohol" for gatherings.

Everything changed after my trip to Taiwan, where I finally crossed the threshold into the deep, aromatic world of single malts. 

## The Turning Point: A Kavalan Bar in Taipei

While traveling in Taiwan, I met up with a friend who took me to a local Kavalan whisky bar. Kavalan is Taiwan’s proud award-winning distillery, famous for its rapid maturation due to the subtropical climate. 

As we sampled a flight of different expressions, a revelation hit me. I realized my palate instantly fell in love with **Sherry Casks**. The rich, dried-fruit sweetness, dark chocolate undertones, and warm spices completely rewrote my definition of whisky.

This wasn't just alcohol anymore; it was an experience.

---

## The Duty-Free Haul: Taipei Airport (TPE)

On my way back home, walking through the Taipei Airport duty-free shop, I knew exactly what I wanted for my first *intentional* whisky purchase. No more random buying. I was on a mission for Sherry.

I ended up bringing home two absolute masterpieces:

### 1. Kavalan Triple Sherry
A magnificent release combining Oloroso, Pedro Ximénez (PX), and Moscatel sherry casks. It delivers a velvety texture packed with berries, rich toffee, and a beautiful oak finish.

![Kavalan Triple Sherry](https://www.kavalanwhisky.com/data/goods/cover/1637303747427196532.png)

### 2. Macallan 12 Year Old (Color Collection)
The "Rolls-Royce of Whiskies" meets travel retail excellence. This edition celebrates the natural color imparted by seasoned European oak sherry casks, offering notes of sweet citrus, orchard fruits, and gentle wood spice.

![Macallan 12 Color Collection](https://tony-almanac.vercel.app/images/posts/whisky/macal12color.png)

## Moving From Burn to Appreciation

Just like learning to use a Glencairn glass to concentrate aromas, discovering your preferred cask profile changes the game. For me, it was Sherry. Drinking these bottles isn't about the "throat burn" anymore—it’s about unpacking layers of history, craftsmanship, and flavor.

> "Your first real whisky isn't the first bottle you buy; it's the first bottle you truly understand."
